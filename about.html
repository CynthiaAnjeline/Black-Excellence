Few Words About Black Excellence Nice to Meet You

Black excellence Photography was founded on 12th December 2019. It has been a journey of major learning and experiencing new challenges about the photography industry both locally and internationally. We have met some of the best photographers in Kenya who have made a great impact to B.E.P (Black Excellence Photography) and of course picking one or two along the way. Black Excellence Photography’s name was mainly inspired by Black and White images captured by photographers globally. Black and White images according to B.E. P’s perception captures a lot of attention in terms of art and it also brings more authenticity to an image. Most people tend to relate themselves with black and white images, more like it attracts the inner soul of an individual.

In as much as B.E. P’S main objective is about black and white images, we still haven’t focused ourselves on it. We’re trying to diversify ourselves in the all the categories. We have done Black and White Photography, Conceptual, Events (Weddings, Baby showers, Baby Shoots, Birthdays, Graduations, Bridal showers etc.), Architectural Photography, Branding Photography, Street Photography, Portrait Photography, Nature/Wildlife Photography. There’s still a lot to explore as time goes by and we’re ready to explore all categories as we specialize.

Where magic is happening My Studio

At B.E.P we abide by a French saying ‘QUI VIVRA VERA’. It basically means Time Will Tell. To further rephrase it; It means No matter how hard we try to predict the future, only time will tell. When capturing an image, it becomes more valuable as time passes because with it, it holds a moment, an emotion, a day, a certain year, and an activity, of which it’s always a special memory.
